# Design Standards (Anti AI-Slop)

1. **Typography-driven**: Design relies on strong typography (Inter) over excessive imagery.
2. **Whitespace**: Generous, purposeful padding and margins.
3. **Editorial**: Museum-quality presentation.
4. **Animation**: Meaningful only. No random bouncing or shaking. Use `transform` and `opacity`.
5. **NO FAKE CONTENT**: Do not generate random stock images or fake statistics.
