# Terminology

- **HU**: Habib University.
- **Preferred Partner**: A brand or business that has an official partnership to offer discounts/services to HU students.
- **Offer**: A specific discount, BOGO, or deal provided by a Partner.
- **Brand Portal**: The frontend area where Partners manage their own profile and offers.
- **Admin Dashboard**: The internal CMS used by HU staff to manage the platform.
